A note on ordering new items

All items unavailable through the NYU ordering system require a quote for ordering. The NYU ordering system includes:

Punchouts for: Fisher/ThermoFisher, Millipore Sigma, Staples, Henry Schein, and Grainger.

NYU also has an internal catalogue with items from:

Cell Signaling Technology, Qiagen, Santa Cruz, Proteintech, Agilent, Beckman, Bio-rad, Crystalgen, Fortis, New England Biolabs, Takara, USA Scientific, and Abcam.

I can usually request the quote but the turnaround time from the vendor can vary; it is typically less than a day, but can also take several days. This is only true for items we haven't ordered before, since we can reuse quotes.
